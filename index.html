Cheng Tang

Co-founder and Team Captain at UWMedTechResolve - Medical Engineering Student Design Team aiming to improve the health care system and currently focusing on automating the triage process.

Research Interests

My research focuses on several key areas in robotics:

  • Kinesthetic Teaching for Robot Arms: Developing intuitive methods for instructing and collaborating with robotic arms in diverse environments.
  • Social Robot Design: Creating hardware and behavioral patterns for safe human-robot interaction, integrating extended reality for enhanced user experience.
  • Soft Robots in Human-Robot Interaction: Designing adaptable and user-friendly soft robots, including social companion robots with adaptive learning.
  • Soft Robots in Medical Applications: Focusing on emergency response and surgical procedures to improve efficiency, precision, and patient outcomes.
